Est. 2016

Nathan met Ji walking back from the harvard forest following an immersive vision quest on a brisk September afternoon. After building a small fire together,they decided to grab lunch – grilled cheese at Roxy's foodtruck. Nathan told Ji about how to make almost anything and his love for crafts. Ji was intrigued when he said he has never been bored his whole entire life. This first date lead to many more for the next ten, adventure-filled years.

The proposal

On June 29, 2022, Nathan led Ji to a familiar path during one of their walks. To Ji's surprise, Nathan proposed infront of the forest where they first met nine years ago. After a long, teary interlude, she said yes.

Wedding day

The two decide to get married near the Crane Estate which overlooks one of New England's most coveted beach. They've enjoyed some of the best summers here, surrounded by the beautiful marshes and wetlands. The wedding will take place at The Barn on the Crane estate, located just a few steps from the castle.
