Est. 2014

Nathan met Ji walking back from Norton’s Woods, following an immersive vision quest on a brisk September afternoon. Having not set the woods on fire, they decided to grab lunch – grilled cheese at Roxy's foodtruck. As they sat and ate in front of the Science Center, Ji wondered whether she would make it through lunch without being subjected to a lecture on digital fabrication (she would not). Ji was intrigued when Nathan said he had never been bored in his whole entire life. This first date led to many more over the next ten adventure-filled years.

The proposal

On June 29, 2022 (Ji's birthday), Nathan led Ji to a familiar path during one of their walks. To Ji's surprise, Nathan proposed in front of the woods where they had first met eight years before. After a long, teary pause, she said yes.

Wedding day

The two will be married in Ipswich, MA, amidst the beautiful beaches and wetlands where they've enjoyed some of their most memorable summer moments. The wedding will take place at The Barn on the Crane Estate, located just a few steps from the castle.
